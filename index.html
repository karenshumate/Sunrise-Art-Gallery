Children's Fine Art Classes

Louisville Visual Arts is shaping the next generation of creative leaders by providing quality instruction to 1,000 + students annually through Children's Fine Art Classes and outreach programs in schools and community centers. LVA aims to engage, inspire, and improve our region through art. Each student is provided a $350 scholarship to attend so that economic status will play no part in a child's participation or placement. A registration fee is paid by those who are financially able to support the program. Louisville Visual Arts
